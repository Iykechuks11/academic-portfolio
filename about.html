About Me & Contact

Guten Tag! Zdravo! Merhaba! Hello! I'm John, a cybersecurity software engineer with a passion for exploring the intricate connections between mathematics, computer science, and information security.

Originally from Nigeria, I currently reside in North Macedonia while pursuing my MSc in Computer Science/Cybersecurity. My academic journey is driven by a curiosity to understand and build secure, reliable systems.

Professionally, I focus on 1-2 key areas like: secure software development, cryptographic implementations, vulnerability analysis. I enjoy tackling complex problems and translating theoretical concepts into practical solutions. You can explore some of my work in the Projects and Publications sections.
